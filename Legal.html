What dose legal mean?

In simple terms legal means relating to the law or permitted by law. Doing something legal means that the government of your country has decided that the thing you want to do is legal, for example doing 100 kilometers an hour on a highway that has been labelled as a 100 kilometre zone. For an example involving a website you must check that none of the information that is on your website is plagiarised or directly copied from another website. If you were to directly copy information the original website may be able to take legal action to remove that information.

How to make sure that your Website is Legal

When checking that your website is legal there are some main factors to consider. You must make sure you tell the person interacting with the website that you will be collecting data and what data you will be collecting. You must also include how, where and when the information is being collected.Once the information is collected it cannot be sold to anyone under any circumstances. You must also protect your clients information. You could do this using these methods. Firewalls, physical access controls, and information access authorization controls to protect your data. All of these can be used together or one at a time to protect your clients info.
